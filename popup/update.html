{addon} Updated

Thank you for updating {addon}. You are now running version {version} on Thunderbird {appver}.

New Features

  • Create new rule:

    • A new rule can now be created from the main window.

  • Convert last email move into rule:

    • The folderset used for the last email move can be inserted into a new rule.

  • Apply rule:

    • In the rule list, the rules can be directly applied without opening the folders in Thunderbird's main view.

  • Composer: Switch to cc or bcc line

    • By keyboard, esc followed by c, the cc line is shown and focused. Similiarly, esc - b opens the bcc , esc-t the to line. Just as in earlier versions, cc (+space), bcc (+space), to (+space) can be used as well (disabled in 2.2.1).

  • Composer: Always show cc or bcc line

    • In the preferences, you can choose to have cc and/or bcc line always visible

  • XNote++: Adding email - notes by keyboard

    • You might try the XNote++ addon: we have added the capability to add notes to emails by keyboard.
